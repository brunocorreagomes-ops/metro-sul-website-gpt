Signal 02 / artist statement

Electronic music moving through the pressure between time and pulse.

Metro Sul moves through nocturnal city energy, gravity fields, late transit, and the emotional residue of motion. The sound is cinematic and physical: synthetic pulse, orbital tension, voltage, memory, and release.

Transmission / MS-04 Southern signal. Vertical motion. Afterimage rhythm.

Built for headphones, night drives, kinetic rooms, and the strange seconds when the city starts to feel weightless.
