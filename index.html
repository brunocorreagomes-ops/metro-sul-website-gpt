Signal 02 / Artist statement

The clock, the commute, the dance floor, the return.

Metro Sul is an electronic music project built around the urban cycle: pressure, transit, release, immersion and return. Its sound moves between the mechanical discipline of Chronos and the suspended intensity of Kairos — from dry machine rhythms and anxious pulses to deep nocturnal grooves, club atmospheres, blurred city lights and emotional decompression.

Sound coordinates / MS-04 The moment the clock stops controlling the body.

Metro Sul transforms urban time pressure into electronic motion — from the rigidity of the workday to the suspended pulse of the night.
